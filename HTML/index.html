About Us
Welcome onboard to "DoctorAid" a platform revolutionizing healthcare accessibility and organization for families and healthcare professionals in Sri Lanka. In today’s fast-paced world, finding timely and efficient healthcare remains a challenge. Families often struggle to locate nearby doctors, schedule appointments efficiently, and manage medical records. Meanwhile, doctors rely on outdated methods like manual appointment systems and physical record-keeping, resulting in inefficiencies and poor communication. DoctorAid bridges this gap by offering an all-in-one, user-friendly healthcare solution that connects families with trusted medical professionals, streamlines appointment management, and ensures seamless, secure handling of medical records. With DoctorAid, healthcare becomes simpler, faster, and more organized for everyone.
What we offer

For Patients

  • Browse and connect with trusted General Practitioners near you.
  • Book Appointments effortlessly and avoid long queues.
  • Manage your family’s health records with a single account.
  • Receive prescription updates and reminders.

For Doctors

  • A comprehensive dashboard for managing appointments and patient records.
  • Streamlined prescription issuing and secure communication with patients.
  • A customizable interface to enhance workflow efficiency.
Empowering Healthcare Professionals with a Smarter Dashboard
DoctorAid-Doctor Dashboard
The DoctorAid platform features a comprehensive and customizable Doctor Dashboard designed to simplify and enhance daily workflows for healthcare professionals. With tools to manage appointments, securely store and access patient records, and issue digital prescriptions, the dashboard streamlines clinic operations effortlessly. Doctors can easily track medical histories, monitor allergies, and maintain organized schedules with a single, intuitive interface. By reducing manual paperwork and administrative burdens, the Doctor Dashboard allows doctors to focus on what truly matters – delivering personalized, efficient, and high-quality care to their patients.
Your Healthcare at Your Fingertips
DoctorAid for Patients
The DoctorAid Patient App offers a seamless, user-friendly experience to manage your healthcare needs effortlessly. From browsing trusted doctors nearby and booking appointments with just a few taps to organizing your family's medical records in one place, the app ensures convenience like never before. Patients can receive prescription updates, reminders, and direct communication with their doctors for follow-ups. Whether it’s tracking medical histories, scheduling appointments, or staying updated with notifications, the Patient App empowers individuals and families to take control of their health with confidence and ease.
