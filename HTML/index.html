About Us
Welcome onboard to "DoctorAid" a platform revolutionizing healthcare accessibility and organization for families and healthcare professionals in Sri Lanka. In today’s fast-paced world, finding timely and efficient healthcare remains a challenge. Families often struggle to locate nearby doctors, schedule appointments efficiently, and manage medical records. Meanwhile, doctors rely on outdated methods like manual ppointment systems and physical record-keeping, resulting in inefficiencies and poor communication. DoctorAid bridges this gap by offering an all-in-one, user-friendly healthcare solution that connects families with trusted medical professionals, streamlines appointment management, and ensures seamless, secure handling of medical records. With DoctorAid, healthcare becomes simpler, faster, and more organized for everyone.
What we offer

For Patients

  • Browse and connect with trusted General Practitioners near you.
  • Book Appointments effortlessly and avoid long queues.
  • Manage your family’s health records with a single account.
  • Receive prescription updates and reminders.

For Doctors

  • A comprehensive dashboard for managing appointments and patient records.
  • Streamlined prescription issuing and secure communication with patients.
  • A customizable interface to enhance workflow efficiency.
